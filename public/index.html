WELCOME TO THE RAISE ELDERLY ORGANIZATION

The successful prevention of elder fraud innovatively. To reward the people that fight scams, to raise awareness to prevent the success of scams, to investigate and assist in the investigation of scams, to increase safety and security for senior citizens, and to educate the elderly in the best practices to not become a victim of a scam.
